## Policy & Research Communication

Presentations communicating policy, research, and laws to public audiences.

- [Human Ecology Spring 2025 Poster Symposium](/projects/design-communications/human-ecology-spring-2025-poster-symposium/)

  High-level talk on partial history of emissions trading systems in the U.S. (going back to sulfur dioxide and the Clean Air Act), Kyoto Protocol vs. Paris Agreement, polarization in climate change beliefs, RGGI and other current U.S. cap-and-trade systems (not offsets) and their effectiveness in setting limits, funding climate justice, and supporting affordability (through allowance auctions).
